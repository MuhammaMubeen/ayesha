1. mujhe acha lagta hy tumhara mujhe per haq jatana,
ap ky hony se lagta hy zinda hon mien yar .

2. Meri aam si zindagi moen bohot khas ho tum ,
Jesy raat ky liye chand zaruri hy or jesy chand ky liye asman ka hona .

3. Jab Ap dekhti hai nazar bhar ke mujhe,
Lagta hai dua meri asar kar gayi tujhe.

4. Ap ky saath guzre har pal mein sukoon hai milta,
Ap ky bina mien esy jesy samandar ho pani ky bina.

5. Har raat khwabon mein Ap ka hi chehra Nazar aata hai,
Jesy mujhe khwab mien jannat ka anna or zindagi ki wajha ban janna.

6. Allah ne sab kuch diya mujhe ,
Kiun ky ap mily mujhe to har khushi har tamana har hajat hooyi puri meri.

7. Tu meri dhadkanon ka ghehra raaz hai,
Meri sari zindagi mien ap ka ka hona Mery zinga hony ka raaz ka hy .

8. Tere bina sab kuch adhura lagta hai har rang be rang lagta hy ,
Tu hai to kala rang kia zindagi bhi rangien lagti hai.

9. Tere naam se meri har subah hoti hai,
Har raat teri baton se mehkti rehti hai.

10. Tu meri duaon ka sabse khoobsurat jawab hai,
Meri duniya ka sabse haseen Insan hai jis se mujhe pyar hy pyar hy.
